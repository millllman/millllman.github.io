Working peculiarities
Before making contract with us, the designer does a test case for demonstrating his or her skills. We make a contract only when the client understands that our designer is appropriate for him or her. It is usual practice, when the test case is made by several designers for you to choose the best one.
The minimal term of a contract about outstaffing (personnel leasing) is 1 month. If the designer falls sick or quits the job, or you are displeased with his/her qualification, then we undertake to replace him/her as soon as possible. It is mentioned in the contract.
